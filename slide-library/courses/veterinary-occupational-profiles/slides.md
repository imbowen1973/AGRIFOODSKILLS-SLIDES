## Specialist Veterinary Clinical Educator

<div class="twocol">

<div class="col">

### Role Definition

A veterinary clinician-educator is a practising veterinarian who combines clinical practice with the supervision and education of postgraduate trainees, including new graduates, residents, and interns.

They operate within accredited training programmes, residency schemes, and structured graduate development pathways, ensuring alignment with professional regulatory standards and institutional frameworks.

### Core Functions

- Oversight of patient care while mentoring learners towards independent clinical mastery
- Delivery of structured teaching sessions
- Conduct of workplace-based assessments and formal appraisals
- Development of inclusive clinical learning environments



</div>

<div class="col">

### Professional Orientation

The role balances functions as coach, mentor, and supervisor while maintaining responsibility for patient care.

Feedback is delivered using principles of radical candour: caring personally while challenging directly.

The role supports learner wellbeing and autonomy and promotes diversity, equity, and mattering within clinical teams.

### Governance and Quality

- Alignment of educational activities with institutional and regulatory frameworks
- Participation in quality improvement and educational scholarship
- Responsibility for ensuring assessments are fair, reliable, and aligned with learning outcomes


</div>

</div>

## Specialist Veterinary Clinical Researcher

<div class="twocol">

<div class="col">

### Role Definition

A veterinary clinician acting as a clinical researcher is a practising veterinarian who combines clinical practice with clinical research activity.

### Core Functions

- Design, conduct, and oversight of clinical studies involving client-owned animals
- Translation of clinical problems into answerable research questions
- Development of robust study protocols
- Compliance with ethical and regulatory requirements

</div>

<div class="col">

### Research Practice

- Data collection and interpretation in collaboration with data scientists
- Synthesis of evidence to inform clinical practice
- Critical evaluation of literature and participation in peer review

### Capacity Building

- Introduction of interns and residents to research methods
- Supervision of small-scale clinical research projects as part of professional development



### Translation and Impact

- Translation of new research into actionable change across the agrifood sector
- Use of implementation science approaches

</div>

## Veterinary Biosecurity Strategist

<div class="twocol">

<div class="col">

### National Disease Architecture

- Designs comprehensive biosecurity frameworks
- Integrates surveillance, borders, and rapid response
- Balances health, trade, and economic priorities

### Global Disease Intelligence

- Monitors emerging pathogen patterns worldwide
- Provides early warning for disease threats
- Collaborates with OIE, FAO, WHO networks

### Evidence-Based Policy Development

- Conducts strategic risk assessments
- Translates epidemiological data into policy
- Develops import regulations and zoning strategies




</div>

<div class="col">

### Trade & International Compliance

- Provides technical expertise for trade agreements
- Maintains market access during disease events
- Ensures SPS agreement alignment

### Emergency Preparedness Systems

- Develops national contingency plans
- Conducts simulation exercises
- Establishes international mutual aid agreements

### Resource Prioritisation

- Evaluates intervention cost-effectiveness
- Advises on biosecurity investment allocation
- Builds funding business cases



</div>
